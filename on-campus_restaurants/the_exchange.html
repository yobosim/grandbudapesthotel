The Exchange

There is no better place to eat on campus than this cozy little deli located in the heart of the business building. - Anonymous Student

Doesn't mean much, but the Exchange is definitely one of the go-to places for students craving for a decent toasty sandwich. In fact, seating is very hard to find during the busy lunch hours on hectic schooldays.
